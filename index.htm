About

I am currently a Research Scientist at VinAI. Before that, I was a lecturer at HCMUT (2018 to 2020) and a Research Fellow at School of Computing, National University of Singapore (2016 to 2018). I did my PhD in Computer Science at School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS) from 2012 to 2016 under the supervision of Prof. Michael S. Brown. Before joining in NUS, I obtained my Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT), Vietnam in 2010.

My research interests are image processing and computer vision, especially computational photography.
