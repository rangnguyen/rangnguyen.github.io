About

I am currently a Research Scientist at VinAI. Before that, I was a lecturer at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT) from 2018 to 2020, and a Research Fellow at School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS) from 2016 to 2018. I did my PhD in Computer Science at NUS from 2012 to 2016 under the supervision of Prof. Michael S. Brown. Before joining in NUS, I obtained my Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering from HCMUT in 2010.

My research interests are image processing and computer vision, especially computational photography.
